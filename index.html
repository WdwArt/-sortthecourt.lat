About the Game

Sort the Court! is a brilliant indie game that puts you in the shoes of a medieval monarch facing constant decision-making challenges. Created by talented developer Graeme Borland, this game masterfully combines simple mechanics with deep strategic thinking.

Every decision you make as ruler affects your kingdom's population, happiness, and treasury. Will you be a benevolent leader who prioritizes citizen welfare, or a shrewd ruler focused on economic growth? The choice is yours, but choose wisely – your subjects are watching.

How to Play

Master the art of royal decision-making with these simple guidelines.

1

Listen to Petitioners

Your subjects will approach you with various requests, complaints, and suggestions. Pay attention to their words carefully.

2

Make Quick Decisions

Choose to approve or reject each request with a simple yes or no. Trust your instincts, but consider the consequences.

3

Monitor Your Kingdom

Keep track of your population, happiness levels, and treasury. Balanced growth is key to a successful reign.

4

Adapt and Learn

Learn from the outcomes of your decisions and adapt your ruling style to overcome new challenges.
